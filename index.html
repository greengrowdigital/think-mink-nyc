SHOP / FRONTALS & CLOSURES

Frontals & Closures.

13x4 frontals and 4x4 / 5x5 closures, hand-tied on transparent HD lace.

WHAT'S THE DIFFERENCE

Frontal vs closure — pick what you part.

Frontal (13×4)

Ear-to-ear coverage. Lets you part anywhere from temple to temple and pull into ponies. Best for high-style versatility and full sew-ins.

Closure (4×4 / 5×5)

A square piece for the crown. Quicker to install, less lace to maintain. Best if you part the same way every day.

HD lace

Transparent, undetectable lace that disappears on every skin tone. Knots can be bleached for a flat melt.

Lash aftercare.

  • No water on lashes for 24 hours after your set.
  • Brush gently every morning with a spoolie.
  • Avoid oil-based products — they break down the adhesive.
  • Sleep on your back or use a silk pillowcase.
  • Fill every 2–3 weeks to keep them looking new.
